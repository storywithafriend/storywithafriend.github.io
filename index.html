How to Play

Play with a friend to write a hilarious story together

  • Pass the device back and forth, writing 2 lines at a time
    • ...
  • You only get to see your partner's last line!
  • Reveal at the end to see your funny creation

Tips:

  • This game is played by 2 friends in a room on a single device. Write 2 stories at a time, passing back-and-forth 2 phones
  • The prompt is optional. You may decide to write about whatever you want!
